ABOUT

We founded Legit Productions way back when in 2013. A simpler, humbler and more peaceful time all round. Since then, our organisation has grown to include two production labels, a video distribution network, a literary publishing wing and now, a film festival. Now a lot of people have asked us why we would start a film festival in the middle of a global pandemic. The answer is, we had no choice. By and large, this whole operation is a way for us to recuperate on some extremely heavy losses this year, thanks in part to the irrational spending habits of our disgraced former CFO Daniel Smith. Nonetheless, the show must go on, as the old adage says. So join us for a festival of forward-thinking filmmaking.
